The Director

Minimalist fine art expressionism

Aether is the experimental studio of Christian Vance, a visual artist and fashion director working out of Copenhagen. Specializing in high-contrast analog monochrome photography and landscape topography.

His work challenges the boundaries between physical shapes and negative space. Selected editorials have been featured in Vogue Italia, Kinfolk, and Wallpaper* magazine.
